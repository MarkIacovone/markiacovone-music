MAI

Born and raised on October 12th of 1996 in La Plata, Argentina, I've played and created music during my whole life while exploring different instruments such as piano, guitar, drums, and my voice itself, resonating with musical pieces made by my favorite artists and influences that would deeply reach my heart, and creating my own music as well. So until now, I've been painting every precious moment of life with melodies and rhythms.

I always looked beyond my country's frontiers and always looked up to having a peaceful and enjoyable life mixed with the inevitable passion towards having music as a living.
